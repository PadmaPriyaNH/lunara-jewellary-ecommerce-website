Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How long does shipping take? +

We process and ship all orders within 1-2 business days. Standard shipping within India takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for an additional fee and delivers within 1-2 business days.

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 7-day return policy for all unworn items in their original packaging. Items must be in perfect condition with all tags attached. Returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return.

How should I care for my jewelry? +

To maintain the beauty of your jewelry, avoid contact with water, perfumes, lotions, and harsh chemicals. Store each piece separately in a soft pouch or jewelry box. Clean with a soft cloth and mild soap solution when needed. Remove jewelry before swimming, bathing, or exercising.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship internationally to select countries. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for more information about shipping to your country. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes.

What materials do you use? +

Our jewelry is crafted using premium materials including 925 sterling silver, 18k gold plating, and ethically sourced gemstones. All pieces are hypoallergenic and nickel-free. We are committed to sustainable and responsible sourcing practices.

Return Policy

Our hassle-free return process

7-Day Return Window

You have 7 days from the date of delivery to return your item for a refund or exchange.

Return Conditions

  • Items must be unworn and in original condition
  • All original packaging and tags must be included
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery

Note: Earrings cannot be returned for hygiene reasons unless faulty.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team via email or phone
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. You will receive a return authorization and shipping label
  4. Package the item securely and ship it back
  5. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Refunds

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days. A 15% restocking fee applies to all returns.

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
